Aim and Objective




The main aim of voluntary blood donation is to ensure the availability of safe and quality blood and blood components round the clock and throughout the year. This helps alleviate human suffering, even in remote areas.

The major objectives are:

  • To wipe out the scarcity of blood and ensure its availability
  • To inculcate the values of caring for others and responsible citizenship among the donors

Blood donation


The key benefits of blood donation include:

  • Saving lives by providing essential blood and blood components
  • Inculcating values of altruism, social responsibility and healthy lifestyle among donors
  • Donors get a sense of personal satisfaction, self-esteem and pride
  • health benefits as donors are screened for health conditions
